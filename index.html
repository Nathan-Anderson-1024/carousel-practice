The Life Cycle of a Caterpillar

The Beginning

The caterpillar starts off as an egg on a leave, yet to be exposed to the outside elements.

Young Life

The caterpillar has hatched! It then spends it's time eating and preparing for what will come next.

Metamorphosis

The caterpillar has eaten enough to make a cocoon where it will be hard at work transforming.

Awaken

The caterpillar awakens from it's cocoon...but wait its not a caterpillar anymore! It is now a butterfly!

The End

The caterpillar lives out the rest of its life a beautiful butterfly flying in the gardens.
